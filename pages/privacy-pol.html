Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how uiVault collects, uses, and protects your data.

1. What Information We Collect

uiVault collects only the minimum data required to operate the platform smoothly.

  • Basic device & browser information
  • Payment transaction reference (no card details)
  • Component unlock status

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • No passwords
  • No card or UPI details
  • No private personal identity data

3. Payments & Security

All payments are processed via trusted gateways (like Razorpay). uiVault never stores your sensitive payment data.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We may use lightweight cookies or local storage to remember unlocked components and improve user experience.

5. Third‑Party Services

uiVault may rely on third‑party services strictly for payments or analytics. These services follow their own privacy policies.

6. Your Control

You are always in control. If you have privacy concerns, you can reach us anytime.

7. Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated to reflect platform improvements. Changes will be effective immediately after posting.
